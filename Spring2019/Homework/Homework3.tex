\section{}  Show that an open ball $B_n=\{x\in \bR^n| ~|x|<1\}$ is diffeomorphic to $\bR^n$ by constructing a smooth bijection map.


\section{}  For elements $x=(x^0,\cdots,x^n)$ and   $y=(y^0,\cdots,y^n)$ of $\mathbb{C}^{n+1}\backslash \{0\}$ , we define an equivalence relation $x \sim y$ by
$$
x=\alpha y  \qquad \alpha \in \mathbb{C}~.
$$
Let us define $\mathbb{C}P^n$ by $(\mathbb{C}^{n+1}\backslash\{0\})/\sim$. Show that $\mathbb{C}P^n$ is a manifold and
$\bC P^1$ is diffeomorphic to $S^2$. The space is called a complex projective space.



\section{}  For a positive integer $d$, we define a map $f^{(d)}:\bC\to \bC; ~z\mapsto z^d$. Let $z=x+iy$ \((x, y \in \mathbb{R}),\) and consider \(f^{(d)}\) as a function of \(x\) and \(y .\) Compute the Jacobian matrix of \(f^{(d)}\).



\section{}   Show that the tangent bundle $TS^1$ of a circle $S^1$ is diffeomorphic to $S^1\times \bR$.

Lecture videos

Course Materials

The first part of this course focuses on 2d CFT, based on my lecture note. The latter part will cover CFT in higher dimensions, for which I follow 3 notes.

  1. Yu Nakayama, An Invitation to Higher-Dimensional Conformal Field Theory (SGC Library Vol. 153), written in Japanese.
  2. David Simmons-Duffin, Phys 229ab Advanced Mathematical Methods: Conformal Field Theory.
  3. Slava Rychkov, EPFL Lectures on Conformal Field Theory in D ≥ 3 Dimensions.

Due to copyright restrictions, I will not be uploading the lecture notes publicly for higher-dimensional CFT.
